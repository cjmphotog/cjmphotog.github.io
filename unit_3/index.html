12 stories told through portraiture, from the heart of Austin's music ecosystem

Heart of the City is part picture, part story, and part outreach on behalf of musicians, music industry professionals, and their family members who are hard at work making a living and making art within the changing dynamics of our growing city. This project is an artistic collaboration between the SIMS Foundation, photographer Carlos J. Matos, and Ovrld Magazine.

This website is a work in progress. For more information contact Carlos J. Matos.
